Recruit with us

Architectural Elite strives to give you the best service possible. Here's a bit more about what we do for you;

Managed Advertisements

We can manage your whole advertisement for you from giving advice on which media to use as well as doing the design and dealing with the response from the advert so you can get on with work.

Specialised CV search

Architectural Elite have a state of the art database system in which we can quickly and thorough search our database of over 75,000 candidates and give a match on your criteria.

Selection of candidates to choose from

Once we have done our searches, own advertising and any worked through our social networks we will provide you a selection of candidates to choose from that match your criteria.

Assist with interview tips

If you haven't had a lot of experience interviewing or you would like to brush up on your techniques we can give you tips on how to conduct your interview.

Executive search/Headhunting assignment

Architectural Elite's recruitment consultants are also experienced headhunters so if you have a high profile or specialist role to fill we would assign a recruitment consultant to you who could manage the process and headhunt those elite candidates for you.

Meetings to discuss requirements

We are constantly meeting with our client's to discuss future business and their requirements. We will arrange a time and day convenient to you either via phone, through the internet or face to face.

Salary guides

The architectural and engineering market are changing constantly so we can provide you with a tailor made salary guide for you, taking into account, candidate's seniority, location and sector.

Manage the candidate through the process

It is important to manage the candidate through the interview and offer process and we are experienced at doing so in order to keep the candidate aware of what is going on every step of the way.

Most of all what we pride ourselves on is a professional and long lasting relationship with our clients.
